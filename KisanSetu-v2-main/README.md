# KisanSetu – The Farmer’s Bridge

## Overview
**KisanSetu** is a multi-role agricultural digital marketplace designed to directly connect **Farmers, Merchants, and Customers** in a unified ecosystem.  
The platform eliminates middlemen, enables transparent price discovery, improves farmer profitability, and provides customers with direct access to fresh agricultural produce at fair prices.

The system is built as a scalable full-stack platform with role-based dashboards, real-time product listings, price comparison features, and intelligent recommendation workflows.

---

## Core Objectives
- Provide **direct farmer-to-customer connectivity**
- Enable **farmers to compare merchant prices** before selling
- Allow **customers to buy directly from farmers**
- Provide **merchants a transparent procurement system**
- Offer **real-time market visibility** and crop availability insights

---

## Platform Roles

### 1. Farmer Portal
The Farmer Dashboard allows farmers to:
- Create and manage their profile with farm details
- Upload available crops with:
  - Crop name
  - Quantity
  - Expected price
  - Harvest date
  - Crop images
- View merchant demand for crops
- Compare merchant prices instantly
- Accept the best purchase offer
- Track order requests from customers
- Monitor sales history and revenue analytics

---

### 2. Merchant Portal
The Merchant Dashboard enables merchants to:
- Register their business profile and service area
- Post procurement requirements for crops
- Upload available purchase rates for different crops
- View farmer listings in real time
- Send purchase requests to farmers
- Manage procurement orders and delivery schedules
- Track purchase analytics and supplier performance

Farmers can view merchant listings and **compare merchant purchase prices** before selling crops.

---

### 3. Customer Portal
The Customer Dashboard allows customers to:
- Browse available crops directly from farmers
- View farmer profiles, farm location, and crop details
- Compare prices between multiple farmers
- Place direct purchase orders
- Track order status and delivery progress
- Save preferred farmers for recurring purchases

Customers receive **fresh produce directly from farmers**, ensuring better pricing and quality.

---

## Key Platform Features
- Multi-role authentication (Farmer, Merchant, Customer)
- Role-based dashboards with dynamic UI
- Real-time crop listing and availability system
- Merchant price comparison engine
- Customer price comparison across farmers
- Direct ordering and transaction workflow
- Crop image upload and management
- Analytics dashboards for sales and procurement insights
- Scalable modular architecture for future AI integrations

---

## Technology Stack
- **Frontend:** React + TypeScript + TailwindCSS
- **Backend:** Node.js + Express.js
- **Database:** MongoDB
- **State Management:** Redux / Zustand
- **Deployment:** Cloud-based scalable infrastructure

---

## Vision
KisanSetu aims to become a **national agricultural digital infrastructure platform** that empowers farmers with transparent pricing, strengthens merchant procurement systems, and provides customers with direct farm-to-home access — creating a fair, efficient, and technology-driven agricultural marketplace.
